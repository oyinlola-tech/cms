Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-15

This Church Management System (CMS) is used to manage church operations and communicate updates to members and visitors. We collect only the data needed to operate the platform and keep records accurate.

What we collect

  • Member information (e.g., name, contact details, department, joined date).
  • Finance records (e.g., donations, expenses, transaction details).
  • Operational content (e.g., announcements, programs, gallery images).
  • Security/audit data (e.g., login timestamps and IP addresses).

How we use data

  • To administer church activities and maintain accurate records.
  • To provide reports and summaries for decision-making.
  • To secure the platform and prevent unauthorized access.

Contact

For privacy questions, please contact the church office via the Contact page.
